ABOUT US


Welcome to Speak Out, a violence reporting platform designed to empower individuals and communities to speak up against violence. We believe that everyone has the right to feel safe and secure in their environment, and we are committed to making reporting violence easier and more accessible for everyone.

Our team is composed of passionate individuals from different backgrounds, working together to make a difference in our communities. We are driven by a shared vision of a world free from violence, where everyone can live without fear.
At Speak Out, we believe that reporting violence is a critical step towards preventing it. By giving victims and witnesses a platform to share their experiences, we can raise awareness, hold perpetrators accountable, and ultimately, create a safer world for everyone.

Our platform offers a range of features to help users report violence, including safe zones where users can seek refuge in case of danger, and multi-media support for situations where video recording is impeded by tough environments. We are constantly working to improve our app and provide the best user experience
We are committed to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals 5 and 16 , which aim to achieve gender equality and promote peaceful and inclusive societies for sustainable development. By working towards these goals, we believe we can create a world where everyone has the opportunity to thrive. Thank you for joining us on this journey to make the world a safer place. Together, we can Speak Out against violence and create a better future for all.
